HERE COMES THE SUN

Finding light in simplicity and hope

The design idea intends to exploit simple processes such as joints and cuts, which can interact with the light, obstructing or filtering it, so as that make the user’s interaction with the object as simple as possible. The inspiration derived from U.G.I. comes from the idea of giving value to every day through small, pleasant moments and finding a glimmer of light even in difficulties
